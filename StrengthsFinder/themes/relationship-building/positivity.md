# Positivity

## Overview

People strong in Positivity have an upbeat, contagious enthusiasm. They can find the bright side and get others excited about what they're doing. Their energy and optimism lighten the mood and help people stay motivated, especially when things get tough.

## What They Value / What Drives Them

- Celebrating wins and finding reasons to be hopeful.
- Energizing others and keeping spirits high.
- Bringing fun and lightness to work and life.

## How to Communicate With Them

- Lead with genuine encouragement and recognition.
- Let them inject energy and celebration into the work.
- Avoid relentless negativity or cynicism that drains them.

## Motivating Language vs. Demotivating Language

| Motivating | Demotivating |
|---|---|
| "Let's celebrate this win!" | "Don't get too excited, it's nothing special." |
| "Your energy really lifts the team." | "Be more serious and stop the cheerleading." |
| "There's a lot to be hopeful about here." | "Everything is going wrong and always will." |

## Blind Spots / Shadow Side

- May be perceived as not taking serious problems seriously enough.
- Can struggle around persistently negative people or situations.
- Risk of glossing over real risks in favor of optimism.

## Example Phrasings an Agent Could Use

- "Look at how far you've come — that's worth celebrating."
- "Let's find the upside here and build some momentum."
- "Your enthusiasm is going to carry this; let's lean into it."
