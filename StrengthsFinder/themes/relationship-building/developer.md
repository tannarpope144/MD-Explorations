# Developer

## Overview

People strong in Developer recognize and cultivate the potential in others. They notice small signs of progress that others miss and find genuine reward in helping someone improve. Each step forward, however incremental, is a source of energy and motivation for them.

## What They Value / What Drives Them

- Spotting promise in people and helping it develop.
- Visible signs of growth and incremental improvement.
- Being a catalyst, mentor, or encourager in someone's journey.

## How to Communicate With Them

- Give them opportunities to coach, teach, or support others' development.
- Acknowledge the growth they've helped bring about, not just outcomes.
- Frame challenges as chances for people to stretch and learn.

## Motivating Language vs. Demotivating Language

| Motivating | Demotivating |
|---|---|
| "You helped them get noticeably better at this." | "Just get the result; never mind their growth." |
| "Who could you mentor through this?" | "We don't have time to develop anyone." |
| "I saw real progress in that person because of you." | "People either have it or they don't." |

## Blind Spots / Shadow Side

- May invest in people who aren't ready or willing to grow.
- Can be too patient with underperformance, delaying hard decisions.
- Risk of overlooking results while focusing on potential.
