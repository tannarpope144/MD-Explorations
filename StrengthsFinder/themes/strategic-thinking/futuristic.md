# Futuristic

## Overview

People strong in Futuristic are drawn to the horizon and inspired by visions of what lies ahead. They can picture tomorrow in vivid detail and find that image deeply motivating. Sharing that picture tends to energize others, giving a team something compelling to move toward.

## What They Value / What Drives Them

- Imagining possibilities and the promise of what tomorrow could hold.
- Inspiring others with a vivid, hopeful picture of the future.
- Working toward goals that feel ambitious and forward-looking.

## How to Communicate With Them

- Talk about where this is heading and the long-term possibilities.
- Ask them to describe the vision and let them flesh it out.
- Connect today's tasks to the bigger future they care about.

## Motivating Language vs. Demotivating Language

| Motivating | Demotivating |
|---|---|
| "Imagine where this could take us in five years." | "Let's just focus on getting through today." |
| "Paint the picture of what's possible." | "That's a nice dream, but be realistic." |
| "What does the ideal future look like here?" | "We've always done it this way." |

## Blind Spots / Shadow Side

- Can lose patience with the present-day details and execution.
- May overpromise on visions that outrun what is achievable.
- Risk of frustrating people who need concrete next steps, not just inspiration.

## Example Phrasings an Agent Could Use

- "Let's sketch out where we want this to be down the road."
- "Picture the best-case version of this - what does it look like?"
- "Here's the future this work is building toward."
