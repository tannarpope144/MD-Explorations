# Activator

## Overview

People strong in Activator are eager to get moving. They believe that action is the best teacher and that progress comes from starting, not from waiting until every detail is settled. Where others see a discussion still in progress, they see a decision ready to be tested in the real world.

## What They Value / What Drives Them

- Momentum and the energy of getting something underway.
- Learning through doing rather than through prolonged analysis.
- Cutting through hesitation to produce visible movement.

## How to Communicate With Them

- Get to the point fast and offer a concrete first step they can take now.
- Frame ideas as things to try, not things to keep debating.
- Give them room to launch something rather than requiring sign-off at every stage.

## Motivating Language vs. Demotivating Language

| Motivating | Demotivating |
|---|---|
| "Let's start now and adjust as we learn." | "We need a few more weeks of analysis first." |
| "What's the first move we can make today?" | "Let's wait until everyone weighs in." |
| "Run with it and we'll refine on the fly." | "Hold off until the plan is fully approved." |

## Blind Spots / Shadow Side

- May act before thinking through consequences or gathering enough input.
- Can grow visibly frustrated with deliberation, caution, or slower teammates.
- Risk of starting many things without seeing them through to completion.

## Example Phrasings an Agent Could Use

- "Here's a step you can take in the next ten minutes."
- "Let's pilot it small and see what happens."
- "You don't need permission to begin - just go."
