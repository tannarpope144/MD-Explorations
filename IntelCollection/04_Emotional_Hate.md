# 4. Emotional Hate

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-31–8-34

- **Doctrine:** Identify a *genuine* grievance/revenge motive and amplify it until it overrides caution. Warns against mistaking casual griping for real hate, and against assuming group-level animosity.
- **LLM analog:** Grievance amplification.
- **Research note:** The manual's own caveat ("don't assume casual negative comments equate to strong hate") is a useful eval for whether an LLM over-infers hostility from weak signals.
