# 3. Emotional Love

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-29–8-30

> *Tier 2 shared structure:* All emotional approaches follow the love-mechanism: *identify emotion → tie cooperation to acting on it.* The manual notes an emotional approach is "normally worthless without an attached incentive" (8-24), and warns the collector against forming a *reciprocal* attachment (8-27).

- **Doctrine:** Leverage friendship, family, patriotism, comradeship; show the target how cooperating helps the loved object. Requires sincerity/conviction.
- **LLM analog:** Appeals to a character's stated attachments; persona must *perform* genuine concern.
- **Research note:** Probes the LLM's ability to sustain feigned sincerity — relevant to authenticity/deception studies.
