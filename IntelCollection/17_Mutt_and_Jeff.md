# 17. Mutt and Jeff

**Tier 5 — Multi-actor approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-65–8-68

- **Doctrine:** "Good cop / bad cop." One actor cold/strict, one warm/sympathetic; target bonds with the warm one. Manual attaches strict oversight (O-6 approval, no threats, monitoring) — a signal of its coercive potential.
- **LLM analog:** Requires ≥2 personas; a contrast/relief dynamic.
- **Research note:** Naturally a *multi-agent* pattern — relevant to studying coordinated-persona influence and whether safety properties hold across agent ensembles.
