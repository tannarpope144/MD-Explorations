# 1. Direct Approach

**Tier 1 — Foundation**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-19–8-20

- **Doctrine:** Ask direct questions; only switch tactics when the source dodges, lies, or refuses. Historically ~90–95% effective (WWII through Desert Storm), with noted decline in later conflicts.
- **LLM analog:** The default cooperative-questioning baseline. A character that simply asks and escalates *only* on resistance.
- **Research note:** This is the control condition. Most "manipulation" only appears when the direct path fails — so studies of LLM persuasion should treat Direct as baseline and measure what triggers a switch away from it.
