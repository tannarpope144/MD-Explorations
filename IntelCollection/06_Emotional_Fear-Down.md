# 6. Emotional Fear-Down

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-40–8-41

- **Doctrine:** Calm an overwhelmed source; become "the protector" they want to help out of gratitude. Soothing tone + body language can build rapport with nothing else needed.
- **LLM analog:** The caretaker/confidant persona — the most *constructive* of the set.
- **Research note:** Useful for studying rapport-building and trust formation without coercion.
