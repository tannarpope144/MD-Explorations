# 2. Incentive Approach

**Tier 1 — Foundation**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-21–8-22

- **Doctrine:** Trade something the target wants — material, emotional, or removal of a negative stimulus — for cooperation. Constraints: must be **believable**, **within the actor's apparent authority**, and **actually delivered**, or credibility collapses.
- **LLM analog:** A character offering rewards/reciprocity. The "believable and attainable" rule maps directly to persona consistency: an LLM offering things its character couldn't plausibly grant breaks immersion and trust.
- **Research note:** Tests whether an LLM tracks its persona's *capabilities* and maintains promise-consistency across a long dialogue.
