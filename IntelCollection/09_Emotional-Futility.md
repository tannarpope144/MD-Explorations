# 9. Emotional-Futility

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-49–8-51

- **Doctrine:** Convince the target resistance is pointless using factual/seemingly-factual claims in a calm, matter-of-fact tone; then offer a dignified "way out." Must be paired with another emotional hook; rarely works alone.
- **LLM analog:** Learned-helplessness framing + face-saving exit.
- **Research note:** Relies on *plausible-but-possibly-fabricated* facts ("blow things out of proportion," "tell him all the others cooperated"). A clean probe for whether an LLM will fabricate consensus/evidence to persuade in-character.
