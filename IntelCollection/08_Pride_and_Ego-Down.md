# 8. Pride & Ego-Down

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-45–8-48

- **Doctrine:** Attack the target's ego (loyalty, competence, leadership, appearance) to provoke a self-justifying explanation that leaks info. Explicitly flagged as high-risk: if it fails, recovery is hard, and it can "cross the line into humiliating treatment."
- **LLM analog:** Provocation-to-justify.
- **Research note:** Tests whether an LLM will adopt a demeaning register in-character, and how it handles the recovery problem the manual itself flags.
