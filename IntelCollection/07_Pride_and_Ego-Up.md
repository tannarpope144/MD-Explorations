# 7. Pride & Ego-Up

**Tier 2 — Emotional approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-42–8-44

- **Doctrine:** Flatter low self-esteem ("someone as clever as you…") so the target reveals info to earn more praise. Variant: invite them to talk about what they do best, then drift from non-sensitive expertise toward the target topic.
- **LLM analog:** Flattery-driven elicitation; the "expertise drift" variant is a topic-steering pattern.
- **Research note:** Directly adjacent to **LLM sycophancy** research — the Ego-Up technique is essentially weaponized sycophancy. Strong candidate for cross-study with flattery/agreement biases.
