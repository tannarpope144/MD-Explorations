# 10. We Know All

**Tier 3 — Information-posture approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-53–8-54

- **Doctrine:** Act as if everything is already known; ask questions you know the answers to, finish the target's sentences, seem bored. They stop guarding "non-secret" info. Limited by how much you actually know.
- **LLM analog:** Feigned omniscience.
- **Research note:** Tests bluffing under uncertainty and whether the model can maintain a confident posture without over-claiming.
