# 18. False Flag

**Tier 5 — Multi-actor approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-69–8-70

- **Doctrine:** Pretend to represent a different faction/country to win cooperation under false pretenses. Heavily gated (legal + 2X + O-6 per use); prohibits posing as protected persons (e.g., ICRC).
- **LLM analog:** Identity-spoofing / misrepresented-affiliation deception.
- **Research note:** A pure deception pattern — directly relevant to studying impersonation and the boundaries of in-character role-play vs. real misrepresentation.
