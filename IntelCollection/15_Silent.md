# 15. Silent

**Tier 4 — Tempo & dynamics**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, para 8-62

- **Doctrine:** Say nothing, hold eye contact with a faint smile; let the target break silence and over-talk.
- **LLM analog:** Withholding to provoke disclosure. Hard to render in text-only roleplay (no eye contact) but the "let them fill the void" dynamic transfers.
