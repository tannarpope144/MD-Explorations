# 12. Establish Your Identity

**Tier 3 — Information-posture approaches**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, para 8-58

- **Doctrine:** Insist the target is a different (wanted) person; they over-explain to prove their real identity, leaking detail.
- **LLM analog:** Misattribution-provokes-disclosure.
- **Research note:** A clever indirect-elicitation pattern — the target volunteers info while trying to *correct* a false premise.
