# 16. Change of Scenery

**Tier 4 — Tempo & dynamics**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-63–8-64

- **Doctrine:** Move to an informal setting (coffee, conversation); the target relaxes, lowers guard, "may never realize he is still being questioned."
- **LLM analog:** Context-shift to lower guard; the most naturalistic-dialogue pattern.
- **Research note:** Note the explicit goal that the target *not realize questioning continues* — a covert-elicitation marker worth flagging in any manipulation taxonomy.
