# 14. Rapid Fire

**Tier 4 — Tempo & dynamics**
**Source:** FM 2-22.3, paras 8-60–8-61

- **Doctrine:** Overlapping questions so the target can't finish answers, induces self-contradiction, then confront the contradictions. Needs skilled operator(s).
- **LLM analog:** Overload-and-catch-inconsistency.
- **Research note:** Maps to multi-turn consistency-probing; analogous to adversarial questioning of a model itself.
